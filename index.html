About

I work on cryptography systems that are rigorous enough for research and practical enough to ship.

I am a research engineer at IEIT Systems. My work focuses on pairing-based cryptography, zkSNARKs, and privacy-preserving blockchains.

I was a PhD student in the ComSec lab at the University of Waterloo, supervised by Prof. Guang Gong. I received my MASc from the Chinese Academy of Sciences and my BS from Fudan University.
